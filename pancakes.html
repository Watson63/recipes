Recipe: Pancakes

Image source: wikipedia

  • Time: 30 min
  • Serves: 6
Ingredients Quantity
Flour 2 cups
Sugar 2 tbsp
Baking Powder 4 tbsp
Salt 1 tsp
Eggs 2
Milk 2 cups
Melted Butter 2 tbsp

Step 1: Measure flour into mixing bowl.
Step 2: Add sugar, baking powder and salt.
Step 3: Stir well to blend.
Step 4: Combine eggs, milk, and butter.
Step 5: Add to blended dry ingredients and mix's.
Step 6: Heat up frying pan add a little butter and cook pancakes to golden brown on each side.
Step 7: Enjoy!
