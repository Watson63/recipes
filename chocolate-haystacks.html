Recipe: Chocolate Haystacks

Image source: cooking4k.blogspot.com

Time: 15 min
Serves: 6


Ingredients Quantity
Sugar 2 cups
Cocoa 6 tbsp
Butter 1/2 cup
Milk 1/2 cup
Vanilla 1/2 tsp
Coconut 1 cups
Rolled oats 3 cups

Step 1: Boil first four ingredients until it coats the back of a spoon.
Step 2: Add vanilla.
Step 3: Remove from heat.
Step 4: Sir in coconut and rolled oats.
Step 5: Drop onto wax paper.
Step 6: Let cool for at least 1 hour.
Step 7: Enjoy!
