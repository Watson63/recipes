Recipe: Double Chocolate Chip Cookies

Image source: blackwells-butchers.com

Time: 20 min
Serves: 10


Ingredients Quantity
Unsalted butter 1 1/8 cups
Brown Sugar 1 cup
White Sugar 1 cup
Eggs 2
Vanilla Extract 2 tsp
Flour 2 cups
Cocoa Powder 3/4 cup
Baking Soda 1 tsp
Salt 1/2 tsp

Step 1: Preheat your oven to 350 degrees farenhieght.
Step 2: Combine all the ingredents 1 at a time.
Step 3: Roll dough into 3 cm balls.
Step 4: Cook for about 10 minutes or until they are done.
Step 5: Enjoy!
