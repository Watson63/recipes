How to Calibrate a Thermometer

  1. Place thermometer in ice water.
  2. Leave thermometer in ice water till the needle rests.
  3. Observe the temperature showing on the thermometer.
  4. Temperature showing should be 32 degrees.
  5. If showing more or less then reset the thermometer in water. If a needle thermometer then turn the hex nut under the dial face, and the needle will more up or down.
