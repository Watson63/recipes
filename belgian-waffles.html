Recipe: Belgian Waffles

Image source: wikipedia

  • Time: 25 min
  • Serves: 3
Ingredients Quantity
Eggo Yolks 2
Vanilla Extract 1 tsp
Buttermilk 1 cup
Melted Butter 1/4 cup
All-purpose Flour 1 cup
Baking Powder 1 1/2 tsp
Baking Soda 1/2 tsp
White Sugar 1/2 tbsp
Salt 1/4 tsp
Egg Whites 2
Ground Cinnamon 1 pinch

Directions:

Step 1: Preheat your waffle iron.
Step 2: In a medium bowl, whisk together the eggs, vanilla, buttermilk and butter until well blended.
Step 3: Combine the flour, baking powder, baking soda, sugar, alt and cinnamon.
Step 4: Stir into the butter milk mixture.
Step 5: In a separate bowl, whip the egg whites with an electric mixer until stiff.
Step 6: Fold into the batter.
Step 7: Spoon batter onto the hot waffle iron, close, and cook until golden brown. (Waffles are usually done when the steam subsides.)
